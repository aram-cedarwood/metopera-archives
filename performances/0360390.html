Eleventh Grand Sunday Night Concert



Metropolitan Opera House
February 4, 1906

ELEVENTH GRAND SUNDAY NIGHT CONCERT


Rienzi: Overture

Der Freischütz: Schweig schweig
Robert Blass

Schubert: Ständchen
Jessie Minges, Soloist
Paula Braendle, Lucy Lee Call, Jane Freund, Roberta Glanville, Jeannette Herzog, Marion Kingsbury, Lucille Lawrence, Katherine Moran, Inga Örner, Ada Schramm, Estelle Shearman, Louetta Tannert, Edith Vail, India Waelchli, Miss Wambach, Jane Van der Zee
Paul Eisler, piano [First appearance]

La Gioconda: Suicidio
Carmen: Aria [encore]
Olive Fremstad

Böllmann: Variations Symphoniques
Jean Gérardy, cello

Liszt: Hungarian Rhapsody No. 1

Bull: Saeterjentens söndag
Lindblad: Mäntro jo jo
Kjerulf: Ingrids vise
Olive Fremstad
[Fremstad played her own accompaniment to an unidentified encore.]

Der Trompeter von Säkkingen: Behüt' dich Gott
Pagliacci: Prologue [encore]
Otto Goritz

Bruch: Kol Nidrei
Davidov: Am Springbrunnen
Jean Gérardy, cello

Strauss: Wedding Bells

Conductor...............Nahan Franko

According to the original program, the chorus in the Schubert were "Lady Pupils of the School of Opera."
