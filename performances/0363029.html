Nineteenth Sunday Concert



Metropolitan Opera House
March 24, 1918

NINETEENTH SUNDAY CONCERT


Gilbert: Comedy Overture on Negro Themes

Pagliacci: Vesti la giubba
Morgan Kingston

David: La Perle du Brésil: Charmant oiseau
Mabel Garrison

Chopin: Piano Concerto in E Minor
Josef Hofmann, piano

Chiaffarelli: Prelude and Fugue

Handel: Judas Maccabaeus: Sound an alarm
Morgan Kingston

Strauss: Voci di primavera
Emmett: Dixie [Encore]
Mabel Garrison

Rachmaninoff: Prelude in C-sharp Minor
Rubinstein: Barcarolle in A Minor
Moszkowski: La jongleuse
Beethoven/Anton Rubinstein: The Ruins of Athens: Turkish March
Josef Hofmann, piano

Conductor...............Richard Hageman
Piano...................Willy Tyroler

[According to The New York Times, the Comedy Overture by Henry F. Gilbert was "part of a completed first act for an opera, Uncle Remus, that was to have introduced...Brer Rabbit to the stage."]
