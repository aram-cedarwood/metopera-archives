Third Grand Sunday Night Concert



Metropolitan Opera House
December 18, 1898

THIRD GRAND SUNDAY NIGHT CONCERT

Die Meistersinger von Nürnberg: Prelude to Act I

Le Roi de Lahore: Promesse de mon avenir
Henri Albers

Gounod: La Reine de Saba: Plus grand dans son obscurité
Mathilde Bruguière [Only Performance]

Die Meistersinger von Nürnberg: Prize Song
Andreas Dippel

La Damnation de Faust: Voici des roses
Pol Plançon

La Damnation de Faust: Dance of the Sylphs

La Damnation de Faust: Devant la maison
Pol Plançon

Beethoven: Leonore Overture No. 3

La Cenerentola: Non più mesta
Eugenia Mantelli

Paulin: Chanson de mer
Schumann: Wanderlied
Pol Plançon

Rienzi: Overture

Conductor...............Franz Schalk
Piano...................E. Romayne Simmons

SPECIAL NOTICE in the program:
The management regret to announce that
Miss MAUD MacCARTHY (a child violinist)
is still suffering from her trip across the Atlantic, and
will be unable to appear to-night. Her place in the
programme will be taken by
Mons. ALBERS
who has kindly consented to sing another selection.
