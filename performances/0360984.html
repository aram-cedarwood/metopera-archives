CONCERT

Die Walküre: Winterstürme
Riccardo Martin

Rigoletto: Caro nome
Bernice de Pasquali

Flégier: Le cor
Leon Rains

Songs

Bernice de Pasquali

Liszt: Hungarian Fantasy
Ossip Gabrilowitsch, piano

Elgar: Pomp and Circumstance March

Conductor...............Alfred Hertz
Piano...................Richard Hageman

The New York Times gives the key signature of the Tchaikovsky Piano Concerto as D minor. Another paper and the official program list it as D Major. The three Tchaikovsky piano concerti are in B flat minor, G major, and E flat major.
