Kautsky & Rottonara Brothers designed the set for Act I, Burghart & Co. the others.
Carl Burrian became hoarse and after Act II was replaced as Lohengrin by Andreas Dippel. Because the substitution made the performance run late, Act III, Scene 1 was omitted.
