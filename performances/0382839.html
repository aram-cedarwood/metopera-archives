Ed Sullivan's Toast to the Met



Metropolitan Opera House
November 8, 1953 Telecast

Ed Sullivan's "Toast of the Town" presents:


A TOAST TO THE MET

Hosted by Ed Sullivan

Carmen: Act IV Finale
Risë Stevens
Richard Tucker

Boris Godunov: Death of Boris
Cesare Siepi
Paul Franke

Rigoletto: Quartet
Risë Stevens
Roberta Peters
Richard Tucker
Robert Merrill

South Pacific: Some Enchanted Evening
Robert Merrill

Victor Borge: Dressing down Mozart

The Metropolitan Opera Orchestra and Chorus
Non-operatic guests: Victor Borge and Sam Levenson

[Ed Sullivan televised his entire show from the Metropolitan Opera stage with the studio audience filling the house; the audience in the orchestra and dress circle wore black tie. Although a program does not exist, the selections listed above were photographed in rehearsal.]
