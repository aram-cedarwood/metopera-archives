Le Nozze di Figaro, Lohengrin, La Bohème, Die Walküre, Don Pasquale, Hänsel und Gretel, Faust, Martha, Siegfried, Tosca and Tannhäuser were also scheduled for performance during a two-week San Francisco stand. In the early morning hours of April 18, however, sets and costumes for these productions, as well as for Die Königin von Saba and Carmen, were destroyed by the 1906 Earthquake and Fire, ending the company's spring tour in disaster.
