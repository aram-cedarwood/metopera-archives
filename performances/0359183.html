The performance of Il Barbiere di Siviglia began with Act II. In the Lesson Scene, Sembrich sang Proch's "Deh torna mio bene." She sang Strauss's "Voci di primavera" and Chopin's "The Maiden's Wish" as encores.
Cavalleria Rusticana received nine performances this season.
