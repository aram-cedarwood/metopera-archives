Lohengrin: Act I Excerpt

Lohengrin...............Georg Anthes

Conductor...............Alfred Hertz

[The Lohengrin excerpt began with Elsa's entrance and ran to the end of the act. A company program notes that the performance concluded at 1:25 A.M.]

[Note: An insert in the program read: "Owing to indisposition, Mme. Nordica has been unable to leave Pittsburg and therefore cannot appear this evening. Mme. Gadski has kindly consented to sing Valentine in "Les Huguenots," and Mme. Reuss-Belce and Mme. Schumann-Heink will appear as Elsa and Ortrud respectively in "Lohengrin."]

There was one Gala Performance this season.
