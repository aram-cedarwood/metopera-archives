Metropolitan Opera House
November 29, 1896

SECOND GRAND SUNDAY NIGHT CONCERT

Liszt: Grand Polonaize

Il Barbiere di Siviglia: Una voce poco fà
Carmen: Habañera [encore]
Eugenia Mantelli

Simon Boccanegra: Il lacerato spirito
Pol Plançon

Mendelssohn: Violin Concerto in E Minor, Op. 64: Andante; Finale
Charles Gregorowitsch, violin

Cavalleria Rusticana: Intermezzo

Eva Dell'Acqua: Villanelle
Marie Engle

Die Walküre: Wotan's Farewell and Magic Fire Music
David Bispham

Mendelssohn: A Midsummer Night's Dream: Nocturne

La Sonnambula: Vi ravviso
La Damnation de Faust: Devant la maison
Pol Plançon

Bizet/Sarasate: Carmen Fantasy
Charles Gregorowitsch, violin

Drink to Me Only With Thine Eyes
David Bispham

Tchaikovsky: Marche Slav

Conductor...............Anton Seidl
Conductor...............Louis Saar

Piano...................Amherst Webber

This information comes from announcements and reviews.
Seidl led the Liszt Polonaise.
The other assignments are not known.
Webber probably played accompaniments for the many encores.

Plançon probably sang all his selections in French.
