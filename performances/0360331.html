Fourth Grand Sunday Night Concert



Metropolitan Opera House
December 17, 1905

FOURTH GRAND SUNDAY NIGHT CONCERT

Hérold: Zampa: Overture

L'Africaine: O paradiso!
Jacques Bars

Faust: Jewel Song
Jeanne Jomelli

Mendelssohn: Concerto in E Minor: Andante; Finale
Marie Hall, violin [First appearance]

Don Carlo: O don fatale
Olive Fremstad

Zoellner: Faust: Prison Scene Prelude
Heinrich Zoellner [Only appearance]
The composer conducted this performance of his work.

Weber/Weingartner: Invitation to the Dance

Don Giovanni: Madamina
Marcel Journet

Grieg: Peer Gynt: Sov du dyreste gutten min
Traditional: Neckens polska
Kjerulf: Ingrids vise
Olive Fremstad

Gounod/Wieniawski Fantaisie Brillante on Themes from Faust
Marie Hall, violin

Zoellner: The Sunken Bell: Rautendelein's Lied
Heinrich Zoellner
The composer conducted this performance of his work.

Conductor................Nahan Franko

[Journet is listed in the program as singing an aria from Don Giovanni. Since he sang Leporello this season, we assume he performed "Madamina."]
