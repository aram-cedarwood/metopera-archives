Simon Boccanegra received ten performances this season.
The production was originally designed and directed for Lyric Opera of Chicago by Pier Luigi Pizzi, who attended some rehearsals for the Metropolitan Opera staging of the '84-'85 season, but objected to certain costumes and props introduced by the Met's director, Tito Capobianco. At Pizzi's request, his name was withdrawn from the program.
