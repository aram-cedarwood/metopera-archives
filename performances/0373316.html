The Newport Jazz and Folk Festival and a Latin American Music evening were presented at Lewisohn Stadium as part of the concert series. The dates and artists were as follows:

July 12, 1966: The Pennywhistlers, Odetta, Tom Paxton, Judy Collins

July 13, 1966: The Beers Family, Yomo Toro Quartet, Pete Seeger

July 15, 1966: Miles Davis Quintet, Duke Ellington Orchestra

July 16, 1966: Lionel Hampton All Star Reunion with Teddy Wilson, Gene Krupa, Coleman Hawkins, Clark Terry, Illinois Jacquet, Frank Foster, Milt Buckner, Kenny Burrell

July 17, 1966: The Newport All Stars - Bud Freeman, Ruby Braff, Bobby Hackett, George Wein,
Peewee Russell, Edmond Hall; Dave Brubeck Quartet, Jimmy Smith Trio, Thelonious Monk Quartet

July 30, 1966: Latin American Evening: Tito Puente Orchestra with Ruth Fernández and Celia Cruz
