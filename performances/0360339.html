Ring Cycle [31]







The announcements stated: "In response to a widespread demand, and for the convenience more particularly of Students and Holiday Makers, during the CHRISTMAS HOLIDAYS, Mr. Heinrich Conried begs to announce a COMPLETE AFTERNOON CYCLE of Richard Wagner's Music Drama Der Ring des Nibelungen." The four works were performed in five days.
Das Rheingold was performed without an intermission.
Das Rheingold received two performances this season.
